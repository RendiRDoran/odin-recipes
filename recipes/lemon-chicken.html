Lemon Chicken

Lemon Chicken is not a traditional Asian dish, but that does not make it any less delicious. This recipe is something that my husband and I make quite frequently. If you are looking for something that is akin to Asian food without straying too far from an American pallet, this is a great dish. It is especially popular with kids. I like this dish because of all of the citrus flavors that light up your senses.

Ingredients

  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup of light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 6 slices of lemon
  • 1/2 cup finely minced fresh parsley

Steps to Make

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Save remainder marinade. Coat chicken in flour and spices mixture.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  4. Heat oil over medium heat.
  5. Fry chicken until medium brown.
  6. Arrange chicken into 9X13 baking dish and sprinkle with brown sugar.
  7. Mix Broth with remainder marinade and pour over chicken.
  8. Top each breast with lemon slice and sprinkle with parsley.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 20-30 minutes or until tender.
  10. Serve to the hungry people.
