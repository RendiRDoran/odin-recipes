Lasagna

This recipe is easy to follow and delicious for the whole family. It can also be made with just cheese if you have picky eaters in the house. Lasagna is a dish that has been Americanized over the years but still holds a special place in many people's hearts. They think of Sunday dinners at grandma's house, time spent with loved ones, or even a nice dinner at home watching movies. No matter the memory, we can invoke a feeling of celebration and togetherness with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat of Choice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • A Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Two Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Two Cans of Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices: Parsley, Pepper, Dried Basil, Salt, Italian Seasoning, and Fennel seeds
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesean, Mozzerella, and Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the Meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make Ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
